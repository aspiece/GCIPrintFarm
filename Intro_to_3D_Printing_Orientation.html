Introduction to 3D Printing Orientation

This version organizes the opening 3D printing experience into six clear parts. It is designed to help students build background knowledge before they begin printing on the Bambu Lab printers in class.

Class Length:
Flexible opening sequence for your lab
Focus:
Video learning, checks for understanding, and guided practice
Software:
Bambu Studio introduction included
Printers:
Bambu Lab A1, A1 Mini, and P1S
Teacher note: I built this as a ready draft for your repo. Part 1 and Part 4 include one-question-at-a-time checks for understanding. Where you have a specific preferred video, you can swap the placeholder link or embed later without changing the structure.
