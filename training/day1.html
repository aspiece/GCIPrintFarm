Day 1: Foundations of 3D Printing

🎯 Learning Objectives

  • Explain how a 3D printer works layer by layer
  • Identify and describe the key parts of a 3D printer
  • Compare PLA and PETG filaments and explain when to use each
  • Demonstrate how to correctly set up a reusable filament spool
  • Explore MakerWorld and identify a model of interest
