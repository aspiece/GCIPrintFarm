Step 1 of 8 Active 🧑‍🔧 Print Lab Roles
📌 Goal: Know your role before the lab starts.
🖨️ Print Technician
  • Start prints
  • Check the first layer
📦 Materials Manager
  • Check filament levels
  • Help load filament
🔍 Quality Control
  • Inspect finished prints
  • Look for problems
🎧 Helpdesk / Support
  • Help classmates
  • Troubleshoot issues
💡 You may rotate roles during class.
Step 2 of 8 Locked 🖨️ Run the Print Lab
📌 Goal: Start the lab safely and get your print queued.
  1. Clean the build plate
  2. Check the filament
  3. Load your 3MF file
  4. Start the print
  5. Watch the first layer
  6. Ask for help if something looks wrong
Step 3 of 8 Locked 🔩 Know Your Printer
📌 Goal: Identify the key parts of the printer.
  • Nozzle — melts and deposits the filament
  • Extruder — pushes filament to the nozzle
  • Build plate — where the print sticks
  • Filament spool — holds the material
  • X, Y, Z axes — control movement in three directions
🔎 Activity: Find each part on a real printer and point to it.
Step 4 of 8 Locked 🔧 Quick Troubleshooting
📌 Goal: Know what to do when something goes wrong.
  • Not sticking → clean the bed
  • Stringy print → check settings
  • Weak print → increase thickness or infill
💡 Tip: Always ask Helpdesk before stopping a print.
Step 5 of 8 Locked ✅ Before You Print
🖨️ Print Expectations
  • We have 11 printers — you may need to wait
  • Be patient
  • Do not touch other students' prints
  • Work with Helpdesk to submit your print
  • Follow all procedures
