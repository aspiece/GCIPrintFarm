Step 1 of 7 Active 🔍 Get Your Logo
📌 Goal: Find a clean version of your school logo to use as the base of your keychain.
  • Search Google Images for your school logo
  • Choose a simple, clean version — fewer details = better print
  • Download as PNG or JPG
💡 Tip: Simpler logos print better. Avoid tiny text or thin lines.
Step 2 of 7 Locked 🔄 Convert to SVG
📌 Goal: Convert your logo image into an SVG file so Tinkercad can import it as a 3D shape.

First — Sign In to Adobe Express

  • Go to Adobe Express
  • Click Log In → Continue with Google
  • Use your school Google account

🎥 Watch and Follow

  • Upload your logo image
  • Remove the background if needed
  • Download the result as SVG
✅ Checkpoint: Your file should end in .svg
Step 3 of 7 Locked 📦 Import into Tinkercad
📌 Goal: Bring your SVG logo into Tinkercad and resize it correctly.

🎥 Watch and Follow

  • Open Tinkercad → sign in → New Design
  • Click Import → Upload SVG
  • Resize to about 40–60 mm
Step 4 of 7 Locked 🔑 Make It a Keychain
📌 Goal: Turn your logo shape into a printable keychain.
  • Set thickness to 3–5 mm
  • Add a cylinder hole for a keyring near one edge
  • Make sure all parts are connected and grouped
💡 Quick Tips:
  • Avoid tiny details — they won't print clearly
  • Use bold shapes for best results
Step 5 of 7 Locked ⚙️ Export + Slice
📌 Goal: Export your design from Tinkercad and prepare it for printing in Bambu Studio.

Export from Tinkercad

  • Click Export → STL

In Bambu Studio

  • Import your STL file
  • Place flat on the build plate
  • Verify the filament colors are correct before slicing
  • Click Slice
  • Preview the print layer by layer
Step 6 of 7 Locked 💾 Save Your File
📌 Goal: Save your print-ready file with the correct name.
  • In Bambu Studio, save as 3MF
  • File name: LastName_Keychain
✅ Checkpoint: File ends in .3mf and is named correctly.
Step 7 of 7 Locked 🖨️ Print + Reflect

  • Work with Helpdesk to submit your print
  • Do NOT touch other students' prints
